FAQ

Find quick answers to common questions about StockFlow.

01

What is StockFlow?

StockFlow is an API designed for inventory management. It allows users to register, manage products, and track inventory data efficiently.

02

How do I add a new product to the inventory?

To add a new product, log in to your account, navigate to the "Products" section, and click on the "Add Product" button. Fill out the product details such as name, description, and quantity, then save it.

03

Can I edit the product details after adding it?

Yes, you can edit product details at any time. Simply go to the "Products" section, select the product you want to edit, and click on the "Edit" button. Update the necessary fields and save the changes.

04

How do I manage inventory levels in StockFlow?

Inventory levels can be managed by adjusting the quantity of products when receiving new stock or processing sales. Navigate to the product details page, and update the "Quantity" field accordingly.

05

How can I view the history of product transactions in StockFlow?

To view the transaction history of a product, go to the "Products" section, select the product you're interested in, and click on the "Transaction History" tab. This will display a list of all transactions involving that product, including stock additions and removals.
