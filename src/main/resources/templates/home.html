What is StockFlow?

StockFlow is a modern platform that allows you to easily manage your company's inventory. Track products, teams and companies centrally, increasing productivity and organization.

  • Teams and Companies - Organize your team and view company details.
  • Reports - View detailed reports on inventory status and more.
  • Inventory Management - Full control over your inventory, with easy to add, edit and remove products.
